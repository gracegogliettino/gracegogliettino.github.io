abstract: >-
  We investigate regularized quantum information metrics for the scattering process
  $e^-e^+ \rightarrow \gamma, Z \rightarrow \mu^-\mu^+$ with a witness photon
  entangled with the initial state, showing that unitarity provides a consistent
  regularization of divergences in the density matrix and von Neumann entropies.
  We find that entanglement entropy, variation of information, and helicity
  correlations encode equivalent physical information, while the scattering
  cross section acts as a source of helicity and momentum entropy, linking
  quantum information measures directly to observable scattering dynamics.
